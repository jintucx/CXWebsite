HOME APP

Stay updated and connected with colleagues​

Built on Microsoft Teams, Home App is a solution that enables employees in an organization to stay updated and connected with each other. ​

Features:
  • Quickly view and manage shifts and tasks​
  • Chat with team members​
  • Access organization-specific data such as benefits, news, make requests and access forms​
  • View media such as a gallery of photos or important videos/updates​
  • Meet with a featured employee​ ​
